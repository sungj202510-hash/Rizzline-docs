Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to RizzLine ("we," "our," or "us"). RizzLine is an AI-powered dating assistant application designed to help users craft pickup lines, generate conversation replies, and analyze relationship compatibility. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data and how we will treat it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using RizzLine:

  • Text Input: When you use our pickup line or chat reply features, you may input text describing profiles, conversations, or context that you want us to analyze.
  • Screenshots: You may upload screenshots of dating profiles or conversation threads for analysis. These screenshots are processed to extract text content.
  • Conversation Data: When using our conversation analysis feature, you may provide chat transcripts or screenshots of conversations for compatibility analysis.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase RizzLine Pro, we collect payment and subscription information through Apple's App Store, including purchase receipts and subscription status.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us through the "Contact Us" feature, we collect your email address and the content of your message.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, including features used, frequency of use, and error logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered features, including generating pickup lines, chat replies, and relationship analysis.
  • AI Processing: Your text inputs and conversation data are processed by third-party AI services (OpenRouter) to generate responses and analysis. We do not store your conversation content after processing is complete.
  • Subscription Management: To manage your RizzLine Pro subscription, verify payments, and provide access to premium features.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide technical support, and address any issues you may encounter.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and enhance app functionality and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests.

4. Third-Party Services

RizzLine uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter API: We use OpenRouter to access AI language models for generating pickup lines, chat replies, and conversation analysis. When you use our features, your text inputs are sent to OpenRouter's servers for processing. Please review OpenRouter's privacy policy at https://openrouter.ai/docs/privacy.
  • Email Service: For sending feedback through email.
  • Cloud Hosting: For secure data storage and processing.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Conversation Data: Your text inputs and conversation content are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after processing is complete. We do not maintain a database of your conversations or generated content.
  • Subscription Data: We retain subscription information (purchase receipts, subscription status, expiration dates) for as long as necessary to provide subscription services and comply with legal obligations.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we retain your contact information and messages for customer support purposes and to respond to your inquiries.
  • Analytics Data: We may retain anonymized usage analytics for app improvement purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your screenshots are processed locally on your device. Text extracted from screenshots is sent to our servers only for AI processing and is not stored.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request information about what personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your RizzLine Pro subscription through your Apple ID settings or by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the app at any time and delete the app from your device.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Children's Privacy

RizzLine is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

By using RizzLine, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your personal information.
